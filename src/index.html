About


Microelectronics - Microfluidics - Photonics - Neuromorphics - Quantum Chips

Carolina Chip Fab allows students to advance micro manufacturing research and development through rapid prototyping and collaborative engineering. We focus on enabling faster iteration cycles from concept to device, integrating design, process, and test in one agile workflow.

We have engineers working on the design of microdevices using industry standard tools and engineering practices and those working on the fabrication of devices using professional fabrication equipment as well as custom made in-house equipment.
